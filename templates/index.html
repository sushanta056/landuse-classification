Landuse Classification Predictor

Upload a Sentinel-2 GeoTIFF with 10 bands in order: B2, B3, B4, B5, B6, B7, B8, B8A, B11, B12.

“This model is trained using Sentinel-2 data from Kaski and nearby hilly districts. It is best suited for generating land use maps of these regions. Please use raster images smaller than 100 MB, as larger files may take too long to process due to limitations of the free Render.com hosting platform.”

Instructions

  • Your GeoTIFF must contain 10 bands in order: B2, B3, B4, B5, B6, B7, B8, B8A, B11, B12.
  • You can download a sample raster image to try this model:
    • Download Sample GeoTIFF
  • After uploading, click Predict to get the classified map.
  • The predicted file will automatically download once ready.
